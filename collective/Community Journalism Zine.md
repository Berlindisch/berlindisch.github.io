# Berlindisch Zine -  Editorial Layer

## Positioning
A curated, issue-based zine, not a newsletter.

No content spam.  
No weekly updates.

## Core Idea
Deep stories at the intersection of:
- Diaspora
- Food systems
- Logistics
- E-commerce
- Technology

## Lens
Diaspora-first, Berlin-rooted, globally aware.

Focus:
- founders
- operators
- traders
- cultural builders

## Format
- Issue-based (not time-driven)
- Limited release
- Highly curated
- Partner contributions

## Content Types
- Founder stories  
- Supply chain insights  
- Food system narratives  
- Diaspora business journeys  
- Tech + culture intersections  

## Role in Ecosystem
- Builds taste + authority
- Attracts aligned partners
- Differentiates Berlindisch from generic platforms

Depth over frequ
